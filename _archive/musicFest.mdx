## Overview
Music Fest is a music festival that features various genres and artists. They needed a poster design that would attract and inform potential attendees, as well as create a memorable and distinctive brand identity for the festival.

I designed a striking and eye-catching poster that communicates the theme, date, and lineup of the festival, using color, typography and imagery. I also created a logo and a slogan for the festival, as well as a mockup of how the poster would look like in different contexts.

The poster design received positive feedback from the client, the artists, and the audience, and achieved the project goals and objectives.
