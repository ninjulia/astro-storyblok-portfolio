## Overview
After conducting some research and brainstorming, I came up with the concept of using a stylized letter F as the main element of the logo. The letter F is composed of two curved lines that connect at the top and bottom, forming a loop. The loop symbolizes the connection and collaboration between freelancers and clients, as well as the continuous flow of work and opportunities. The loop also creates a negative space that resembles an arrow, pointing upwards and forwards, suggesting growth and progress.

The letter F is also a common abbreviation for freelance or freelancer, making it relevant and recognizable for the target audience. The letter F is also easy to adapt and apply to various branding materials, such as business cards, website, and social media.
