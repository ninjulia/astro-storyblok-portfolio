## Overview
Earth Matters is a nonprofit organization that promotes environmental awareness and action. They needed a new website that would increase their online presence and reach, showcase their projects, campaigns, and impact, encourage visitors to join, donate, or volunteer, and reflect their mission, vision, and values.

I designed a responsive and adaptive website that works across different devices and browsers, using a clear and simple navigation, a consistent and appealing visual identity and branding, and a user-friendly and intuitive layout. I also developed and deployed the website using WordPress as a content management system.

The website received positive feedback from the client, the users, and the industry, and achieved the project goals and objectives.
